### INBRE Summer Scholars

The application due date is Feb 15th for priority review with a March 15th

https://inbre.montana.edu/student-research-programs/statewide_scholar.html

Montana-based, non-MSU-Bozeman undergraduate students who want to do
significant biomedical research at their home institution or at another
institution within the statewide Montana INBRE Network are encouraged to apply
to this program. Research must be conducted in Montana under the guidance of a
faculty mentor at a participating Montana INBRE Network institution. Students
selected for this 10-week program will be expected to commit to full-time
workload (~400 hours) and make a presentation (poster or podium) to report
their findings.

Selected students receive a $7,000 stipend for the 10-week program
If the research will be conducted away from the home institution, limited
travel costs may be covered by Montana INBRE upon request, funding permitting
If the research will be conducted away from the home institution, limited
funding to cover shared dorm room or other economy accommodation for the
specified 10-week program may be covered by Montana INBRE upon request, funding
and housing availability permitting
For students applying to conduct research at UM in Missoula, UM mentors /
institution will be asked to contribute 50% of the above stipend in match
support;  Please contact Sarah Codd (scodd@montana.edu) if interested, as the
UM VPR Office may be able to help with this match commitment  in some cases.
